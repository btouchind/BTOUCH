Who We Are

About BTouch

Smart solutions designed to empower independence and accessibility.

BTouch is dedicated to improving the lives of individuals with disabilities through innovative assistive technologies and inclusive solutions. We believe that everyone deserves equal opportunities, independence, and the freedom to participate fully in everyday life.

Our mission is to develop accessible, affordable, and user-centered products that enhance mobility, communication, and daily living. By combining technology, creativity, and compassion, we strive to remove barriers and create a more inclusive future for all.

Beyond innovation, BTouch works closely with communities, organizations, and individuals to understand real-world challenges and deliver meaningful solutions that make a lasting impact.

Our Story

The BTouch Journey

The Beginning

Where the Journey Started

Behind every product is a person, a challenge, and a story worth hearing. BTouch was born from the desire to listen, understand, and create solutions that address real-world needs rather than assumptions.

From community interactions to hands-on testing, every step of our journey has been shaped by the people we aim to support.

The Challenge

A Problem Worth Solving

Millions worldwide live with limb loss, yet most prosthetics remain prohibitively expensive, mechanically rigid, and disconnected from the user's intent.

We saw the gap — between what technology could offer and what people actually received. That disparity fueled our mission.

The Journey

Shaped by Experience

Every step forward came through learning, testing, and listening. From early concepts to real-world trials, our journey has been guided by the people who use our solutions and the feedback they generously shared.

Each interaction revealed new insights, helping us refine our ideas and create products that are more practical, comfortable, and meaningful in everyday life.
